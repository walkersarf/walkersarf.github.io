Document Checklist

Canada - Visitor Visa

checklist Required Documents

  • Must be valid for at least six months beyond your intended stay. Ensure at least two blank pages.

  • Completed and signed application form. Download from the official website.

  • Bank statements for the last 3-6 months or other financial documents demonstrating sufficient funds to cover your stay.

  • Confirmed flight reservations and hotel bookings for the duration of your stay.

  • If visiting friends or family, a letter of invitation from your host along with their status in Canada.

info Additional Notes

Please ensure all documents are clear, legible, and meet the specified requirements. Scanned copies should be in PDF format and not exceed 5MB per file. Original documents may be required at a later stage. For any questions or assistance, please contact our support team.
